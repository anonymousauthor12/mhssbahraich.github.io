    • Admission Rules:

  • The admission age for Nursery class should be 3+ years.
  • Admission form should be duly filled the parents should accompany the child at the time of admission.
  • Any form without the parents or Guardians Signature will not entertained.
  • Parents should fill in correct Date of Birth of the child and change will be made later on.
  • T.C. and Progress Report from the previous school should be produced before the admission.

    • Test:

  • Written test and interview will be taken for class nursery to Xth four subjects.
  • English, Hindi, Maths and G.K.

    • Withdrawls:

  • To withdraw a pupil a month's notice must be given.
  • No transfer Certificate will be issued unless all the dues are clear.

    • Leave of absence:

  • No student should leave the school during class hours.
  • Every student should be present in school five minutes before the rings.
  • Medical Certificate should be produced in case of medical leaves.
  • The school is not responsible for the student out side the school.
  • All leave applications should bear the sig, of the parents.
  • The management reserves the right to change any of the rules laid down in prospectus with them.

    • Fees:

  • Fees must be paid by the end of every month.
  • After the end of every month a fine of Rs. 30 will be charged per month till the amount is paid.
  • Arrear fees must be cleared before the examination failing which no student will arrear be allowed to sit in exams.

    • School Uniforms & Books:

  • "The list of uniform and Books will be given at the time of admission.

    • Guide Lines to Parents :

  • Maximum co-operation of the parents is desired in order to provide the best education.
  • The student should be sent in time in clean and neat uniform.
  • The parents should see the diary every day and sign. with remark/suggestion.
  • The parents should inform any change of address.
  • Parents are given opportunities to meet the teacher on parents/teacher meeting day or in case of emergancy with prior appointment.
